About Cradle Care

Cradle Care is a digital parenting platform that supports parents from day one and beyond. We provide evidence-based guides on feeding, sleep, hygiene, and development, combined with access to certified medical advisors online or nearby, helping parents make confident, informed decisions for their families.

Founded in 2023 by a team of healthcare professionals and tech enthusiasts, Cradle Care is dedicated to empowering modern parents with practical tools and expert advice. Our mission is to make quality parenting support accessible to all families, ensuring every child gets the best start in life.

Mission

Cradle Care is dedicated to providing accessible, reliable, and holistic care solutions that promote comfort, health, and peace of mind. We strive to connect people with trusted caregivers and resources, embracing technology and empathy to ensure that every act of care truly makes a difference.

Vission

To be a trusted and compassionate companion in nurturing wellness and care, empowering families and individuals to live healthier, happier, and more balanced lives through innovative and heartfelt support.
